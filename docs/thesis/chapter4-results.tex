\subsection{Transaction Mix Compliance}

The observed transaction mix closely matches the TPC-C specification:

\begin{itemize}
  \item New-Order: 44.9\% (target: 45\%) -- 2,074 tx, 99.71\% success
  \item Payment: 43.4\% (target: 43\%) -- 2,004 tx, 99.80\% success
  \item Order-Status: 4.3\% (target: 4\%) -- 197 tx, 100\% success
  \item Delivery: 3.8\% (target: 4\%) -- 175 tx, 100\% success
  \item Stock-Level: 3.7\% (target: 4\%) -- 171 tx, 100\% success
\end{itemize}

Total transactions: 4,621 with 4,611 successful (99.78\% overall success rate).
